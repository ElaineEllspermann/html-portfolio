The Best Movie according to Elaine

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Duke

A charming, understated and completely enjoyable frolic
about how ordinary people can do extraordinary things
that seems doubly startling because, while seeming implausible,
it also happens to be absolutely true.

The Great Freedom

A quiet and carefully constructed story
about the sheer absurdity of the criminalisation of homosexuality,
and about the denial of humanity that criminalisation required.

Top Gun: Maverick

The return of pure, nostalgic blockbusters.
The best aerial action sequences ever amaze even the highest expectations,
not only due to the absolutely insane real stunts but largely because of the
flawless contribution of all filmmaking elements.
